Motion - FPP Framework

Motion is a first-person framework for Unreal Engine 5, made together with my friend Kia Armani. Our goal with the framework was to create a compact but powerful framework, without spreading ourselves thin with too many features.

We like to think we did this one alright.

City of Sarnath

City of Sarnath is the consequence of wanting to play Dungeons & Dragons, but not having anyone to play it with. It's an open-world text-adventure game, where you'll be free to roam around in the cobbled streets of Sarnath. The game is dialogue-driven, and heavily inspired by classic table-top RPGs.

The story and core gameplay is written in Ink, a scripting language developed by Inkle Studio. Once most of the story is finished, I'll bring the scripts over in Unity and create a more polished gameplay experience.

If you're interested in learning more about the project, check out the devblog!
